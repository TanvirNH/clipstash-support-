🚀 Getting Started

  • Open ClipStash anytime with ⌘ ⇧ V
  • Copy anything and it appears in your clipboard history automatically
  • Click any item to paste it, or press Return to paste the selected item
  • Pin frequently used clips so they're always at the top
  • Use the search bar to find any clip instantly

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

ClipStash isn't capturing my clipboard

Make sure Accessibility permission is granted. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility and ensure ClipStash is listed and enabled. If it's already listed, try removing it and adding it again.

The keyboard shortcut isn't working

The default shortcut is ⌘ ⇧ V. If another app is using this shortcut, there may be a conflict. Check System Settings → Keyboard → Keyboard Shortcuts to see if another app has claimed it. Try quitting and reopening ClipStash.

How many clips are stored?

Free users can store up to 10 clipboard items. Pro users get unlimited clipboard history. Pinned items are always preserved regardless of the limit.

Is my clipboard data private?

Yes. All clipboard data is stored locally on your Mac. ClipStash does not send your clipboard contents to any server. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I exclude apps from clipboard tracking?

Open ClipStash Settings and scroll to the Excluded Apps section. Add the bundle ID of any app you want to exclude (e.g., com.1password.1password). Password managers are excluded by default.

Can I restore my purchase on a new Mac?

Yes. Open ClipStash, go to the Upgrade screen, and click Restore Purchases. Your subscription or lifetime purchase will be restored automatically if you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Open System Settings → Apple ID → Media & Purchases → Manage Subscriptions, find ClipStash, and cancel from there.

What's included in the free trial?

The 7-day free trial unlocks all Pro features: unlimited history, AI Smart Tags, custom groups, privacy vault, plain text copy, unlimited pins, and advanced search. No payment required to start.
